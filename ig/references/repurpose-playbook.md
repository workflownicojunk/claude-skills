# Repurpose Playbook: Cross-Platform Content Adaptation

Rules for adapting Instagram content to other platforms without copy-pasting.

## Core Principle

Repurposing is NOT reposting. Every platform has different audience behavior, content expectations, and algorithm preferences. The core insight stays the same; the execution changes for each platform.

## Platform Adaptation Matrix

### Instagram Reel to TikTok

| Element | Instagram | TikTok Adaptation |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Format | 9:16 vertical | Same 9:16, no changes needed |
| Hook speed | 3 seconds | 1.5-2 seconds (faster pace required) |
| Hook style | Polished, on-brand | More casual, trend-aware, pattern-interrupt |
| Audio | Original audio or trending | Trending sound MANDATORY for discovery |
| Caption | 500+ characters | 100-300 characters (TikTok users don't read long captions) |
| Hashtags | 3-5 niche | 3-5 mix of niche + trending (TikTok hashtags fuel discovery) |
| CTA | "Speicher dir das" | "Folge fuer Teil 2" or "Schreib mir" (save CTA less effective on TikTok) |
| Tone | Warm, expert | Slightly more casual, less polished |
| Watermark | None | NEVER post with Instagram watermark (TikTok suppresses) |
| Posting delay | Original post | Wait 24-48h after Instagram post |

**TikTok-specific rules:**
- Re-record the hook if possible (different energy, faster delivery)
- Use TikTok's native text overlay tools instead of pre-edited text
- Trending sounds: even 1 second of a trending sound in the background boosts distribution
- Duet/Stitch: leave room for reaction content (don't fill every second)

### Instagram Reel to YouTube Shorts

| Element | Instagram | YouTube Shorts Adaptation |
|---------|-----------|--------------------------|
| Format | 9:16 vertical | Same 9:16 |
| Hook speed | 3 seconds | 3-5 seconds acceptable (YouTube audience is more patient) |
| Title | N/A (caption is the title) | SEO-optimized title (YouTube indexes titles for search) |
| Description | Caption | Full description with keywords, timestamps not applicable |
| End screen | N/A | Add "Subscribe" end screen element (auto-generated by YouTube) |
| Audio | Any | Original audio preferred (trending sounds less impactful on YT) |
| Caption | 500+ chars | 200-500 characters with keywords |
| Hashtags | 3-5 niche | #Shorts + 2-3 topic hashtags |
| CTA | Save/Send | "Abonnieren fuer mehr" (subscribe CTA is highest value on YT) |
| Posting delay | Original post | Wait 48-72h after Instagram post |

**YouTube Shorts-specific rules:**
- Title is the primary discovery mechanism. Use keywords people search for.
- Example title: "Krafttraining Frauen ueber 40: Diese 3 Fehler vermeiden" (searchable)
- YouTube Shorts can be up to 60 seconds. If your Reel is 30s, don't pad it. Short is fine.
- Add a verbal CTA to subscribe in the last 3 seconds (YouTube rewards subscriber conversions from Shorts)

### Instagram Reel to LinkedIn

| Element | Instagram | LinkedIn Adaptation |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| Format | 9:16 vertical | 1:1 square or 4:5 vertical (LinkedIn favors these) |
| Tone | Warm, personal | Professional, insight-focused, industry-relevant |
| Caption | 500+ chars (warm, direct) | 800-1500 chars (professional, data-driven, thought-leadership) |
| Hook | Emotional or curiosity-driven | Data-driven or contrarian professional take |
| Content angle | "How to do X for your body" | "What the fitness industry gets wrong about X" |
| Hashtags | 3-5 niche fitness | 3-5 professional (#Entrepreneurship, #WomenInBusiness, #HealthTech) |
| CTA | Save/Send | "Agree or disagree? Comment below" (LinkedIn rewards debate) |
| Audience | End consumers (B2C) | Industry peers, business audience (B2B) |
| Posting delay | Original post | Wait 1 week (different audience, no overlap risk) |

**LinkedIn-specific rules:**
- Reframe the content from a business/industry perspective
- Example: Instagram: "Warum Protein-Timing egal ist" becomes LinkedIn: "We spent 2 years telling our clients to time their protein. Here's what the data showed when we stopped."
- LinkedIn rewards long-form text posts even more than video. Consider extracting the insight as a pure text post.
- Tag relevant people or companies only if genuinely relevant (not for reach-hacking)
- The creator's LinkedIn voice should be more professional than Instagram but still warm (not corporate)

### Instagram Reel to Email Newsletter

| Element | Instagram | Email Adaptation |
|---------|-----------|------------------|
| Format | Video | Text + optional GIF/thumbnail embed |
| Content | Full explanation in video | Extract the key insight in 2-3 paragraphs |
| Hook | Visual/audio | Subject line (most critical element) |
| CTA | Save/Send | "Watch the full Reel" with link (drives traffic back to Instagram) |
| Personal angle | Brief in caption | Expand: add personal anecdote or story not in the Reel |
| Length | 15-60 seconds | 300-500 words |

**Email-specific rules:**
- Subject line = the hook, rewritten for email (curiosity or benefit-driven)
- Add a personal story or context that wasn't in the Reel (gives email subscribers exclusive value)
- Include a thumbnail/GIF of the Reel with a play button overlay (drives clicks)
- End with a question to encourage replies (reply rate improves email deliverability)
- Send 24-48h after the Reel drops (avoid same-day to not split attention)

### Instagram Reel to Blog Article

| Element | Instagram | Blog Adaptation |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|
| Format | 30-60 second video | 500-800 word article |
| Depth | Surface-level (time-constrained) | Deep-dive with sources, examples, practical steps |
| SEO | Hashtags | Keywords in title, headers, meta description |
| Structure | Hook > Points > CTA | H1 > Introduction > H2 sections > Conclusion > CTA |
| Sources | Mentioned verbally | Linked, cited (adds credibility and SEO value) |
| CTA | Save/Send | Email signup or product link |
| Visuals | The Reel itself | Embed the Reel + add supporting images/infographics |

**Blog-specific rules:**
- Expand each point from the Reel into a full paragraph with examples
- Add scientific sources/references (links to studies)
- Optimize for one primary keyword (use in H1, first paragraph, and meta description)
- Include the embedded Reel in the article (drives Instagram views from blog traffic)
- Internal linking: connect to related blog posts and product pages

## Adaptation Rules (Universal)

### What to Change for Every Platform

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Hook | Rewrite or re-record for each platform. Never copy-paste the same hook. |
| Caption length | Match platform norms (see matrix above) |
| CTA | Use the highest-value CTA for each platform |
| Hashtags | Research platform-specific hashtags. Instagram hashtags don't work on TikTok. |
| Tone | Adjust formality level (Instagram = warm, LinkedIn = professional, TikTok = casual) |
| Posting time | Research optimal posting times for each platform independently |

### What Stays the Same

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Core insight | The main point/teaching doesn't change |
| Visual style | Brand consistency (colors, fonts, general aesthetic) |
| Quality standard | Never post lower-quality versions on "secondary" platforms |
| Accuracy | Same facts, same science, same claims across all platforms |

## Content Recycling Timeline

| Time Since Original Post | Recycling Action |
|--------------------------|-----------------|
| 0-7 days | NO recycling. Let the original perform. |
| 1-2 weeks | Cross-post to other platforms (TikTok, YouTube Shorts) |
| 2-4 weeks | Repurpose for email newsletter and blog |
| 4-8 weeks | Create a "sequel" or "part 2" on Instagram with new angle |
| 2-3 months | Reshoot the same topic with updated information |
| 6+ months | Full remake acceptable (audience has rotated) |

**Rule:** Never repost the exact same content on Instagram. The algorithm detects duplicate content and suppresses it. Always add something new: a new hook, updated data, a different format (e.g., Reel to Carousel).

## Repurpose Priority by Content Type

| Content Type | Repurpose Priority | Best Platforms | Why |
|-------------|-------------------|----------------|-----|
| Educational / How-to | Highest | All platforms | Evergreen value, works everywhere |
| Transformation / Before-After | High | IG, TikTok, Email | Emotional impact translates well |
| Myth-busting | High | IG, TikTok, LinkedIn, Blog | Contrarian angle works on every platform |
| Product / Promotional | Medium | IG, Email only | Promotional content feels spammy cross-posted |
| Trend / Reaction | Low | IG, TikTok only | Time-sensitive, irrelevant after 1-2 weeks |
| Personal / Lifestyle | Low | IG only | Platform-specific, doesn't translate well |

## What NOT to Repurpose

| Content Type | Why Not |
|-------------|---------|
| Time-sensitive content | "Heute startet..." makes no sense 2 weeks later |
| Platform-specific references | "Schreib GUIDE in die Kommentare" doesn't work on LinkedIn |
| Trend-dependent content | Trends expire. The content becomes cringe. |
| Highly personal stories | May not match the tone of other platforms |
| Underperforming content | If it didn't work on Instagram, it probably won't work elsewhere |

## Workflow: Weekly Repurpose Process

| Day | Task |
|-----|------|
| Monday | Review last week's Instagram performance. Identify top 2-3 performing pieces. |
| Tuesday | Adapt top Reel for TikTok (re-record hook, add trending sound) |
| Wednesday | Adapt top Reel for YouTube Shorts (write SEO title, adjust description) |
| Thursday | Write email newsletter based on top educational Reel |
| Friday | Optional: LinkedIn post based on most insightful/data-driven Reel |

## Cross-Platform Tracking

| Metric | Track Where | What It Tells You |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Views per platform | Each platform's analytics | Which platform amplifies which content types |
| Engagement rate per platform | Each platform's analytics | Where your audience is most active |
| Click-through to website | UTM in Google Analytics | Which platform drives actual traffic |
| Email signups per source | Email platform | Which platform drives list growth |
| Revenue per source | CRM / Stripe | Which platform drives actual sales |

**Rule:** Track cross-platform performance monthly. If one platform consistently underperforms, reduce effort there and double down on winners. Don't spread thin across 5 platforms for vanity presence.
