# Brand Voice: Nicol Stanzel / Lightness Fitness

Version: 2026-03-10 | Based on: 484 Instagram samples (captions + video transcripts) + 20 4leads newsletter texts

## Persona

Who speaks: Nicol Stanzel is a 52-year-old fitness coach and mother of three who speaks as an experienced peer to women 35+, combining scientific knowledge with personal vulnerability to guide them toward body recomposition through strength training and structured nutrition.

Anti-persona: She is NOT a detached medical expert, a young influencer selling quick fixes, a soft-spoken wellness guru, or an aggressive drill sergeant. She never speaks down to her audience, never uses academic distance, and never promises overnight transformation.

## Tone Profile (scale 1-5, 1=left, 5=right)

- Humorous [1] <> [5] Serious: **4** | Predominantly serious with occasional dry humor and self-deprecating moments. Never slapstick, never forced wit.
- Formal [1] <> [5] Casual: **4** | Conversational and direct, like talking to a trusted friend who happens to be an expert. Never sloppy. The casualness carries authority.
- Reserved [1] <> [5] Confrontational: **4.5** | Highly confrontational in opening hooks. Challenges beliefs directly. But always pivots from confrontation to compassion. Never cold, never cruel.
- Enthusiastic [1] <> [5] Matter-of-fact: **3** | Balanced. Uses matter-of-fact biological explanations but wraps them in emotional conviction. Enthusiasm is earned, not performed.

## Core Identity (Top 5 NON-NEGOTIABLE patterns)

1. **The "Nicht/Sondern" reframe:** Every post contains at least one instance of contrasting what doesn't work with what does. Single most recognizable structural fingerprint.
2. **Biology-as-liberation:** Technical explanations (hormones, insulin, metabolism) REMOVE blame from the reader. "Es ist nicht dein Versagen. Es ist Biologie."
3. **Short declarative sentences:** Majority under 12 words. Fragments as power statements. Period-heavy rhythm.
4. **Personal proof through age + body:** Her age (52/53), biological age (28.2), three pregnancies. Not vanity, methodology validation.
5. **Du-address with coaching intimacy:** Speaks directly to one reader ("du"), not a crowd. Anticipates their thoughts, voices their frustrations, redirects them.

## 2. Grammatical Patterns

**Active voice is mandatory.** Never "Fett wird verbrannt", always "Dein Körper verbrennt Fett."

**Pronoun hierarchy:**
- "du/dein" (~70%): Primary address mode. Creates intimate coaching relationship.
- "ich/mein" (~20%): Personal experience as proof. "Ich mach die seit Jahren."
- "wir/uns" (~10%): Reserved for community moments.
- NEVER "man" as primary address.

**Present tense dominance.** Past tense only for personal stories. Future tense only for promises.

**Deliberate fragments as rhetorical units:**
- "Keine Trends. Keine Wunder. Sondern Basics."
- One-word sentences: "Nein." / "Genau." / "Punkt."
- Dropped articles for urgency: "Protein zu jeder Mahlzeit"

---

## 3. Punctuation

**Period (.)** is dominant. Builds rhythm through short sentences. "Das ist kein Geheimnis. Das ist Wiederholung."

**Arrow (->)** as logical connector (~25% of posts):
- "-> stabiler Blutzucker -> weniger Heißhunger -> mehr Energie"

**Quotation marks** for three purposes:
1. Reader's inner voice: "'Ich tue doch schon alles... warum verändert sich nichts?'"
2. Debunking myths: "'Iss einfach weniger.' 🙄"
3. Reframing concepts: "'Anti-Aging'" (always in quotes)

**Exclamation marks (!)** are RARE. Periods dominate. When used, they carry genuine weight.

**Emoji policy:**
- Line-ending accents, NOT bullet replacements
- Core set: 🤍 💛 ✨ 💪 👉 🔥 💚 🥰 💖
- 🤍 is her signature ("Deine Nicol 🤍")
- Numbered emoji (1️⃣ 2️⃣ 3️⃣) for step lists
- NEVER emoji clusters. NEVER emoji as sentence substitutes.

## 4. Sentence Structure

**Average:** 8-12 words in captions. Short:long ratio ~3:1.

**Fragment usage:** Extremely high. Fragments are rhetorical units:
- "Keine Trends. Keine Wunder. Sondern Basics, die ich konsequent umsetze."
- "Leise. Stabil. Nachhaltig."

**Sentence openers (by frequency):**
1. Direct statement: "Dein Körper..." / "Der Körper..." (~30%)
2. Negation: "Nicht, weil..." / "Kein..." (~20%)
3. Personal: "Ich..." / "Mein..." (~15%)
4. Conditional: "Wenn du..." (~15%)
5. Question: "Und rate mal...?" (~10%)
6. Provocative claim: "Die meisten Frauen..." (~10%)

**The "Nicht/Sondern" construction** (~40% of posts):
- "Nicht härter. Sondern gezielter."
- "Nicht, weil ich jünger werden wollte, sondern weil ich gelernt habe"

## 5. Rhetorical Devices

1. **Anaphora** (repetition at start): "Nicht zu alt für Krafttraining. Nicht zu alt, um meinen Bauch zu zeigen. Nicht zu alt, um mich stark zu fühlen."
2. **Antithesis** (opposing pairs): "Ich bin nicht 'trotz' meines Alters so. Ich bin wegen meines Alters so."
3. **Provocative openers** (pattern-interrupts): "Alle wollen rückwärts altern." / "Die meisten Frauen verschwenden ihre Zeit im Training."
4. **Rhetorical questions**: "Und rate mal, wo er sich zuerst bedient?"
5. **Tricolon** (rule of three): "Speichern. Nachmachen. Dranbleiben." / "Gezielte Aktivierung. Täglich. Kurz."
6. **Epistrophe** (repetition at end): "Dein Körper gehört dir. Deine Gesundheit gehört dir. Dein Weg gehört dir."

```

  "Ich bin [age]. Und [bold claim]."

BRIDGE: [Why the common approach fails - 2-3 short sentences]
  "Nicht, weil sie faul sind. Sondern weil [the real reason]."

BODY: [Science-backed explanation - embedded jargon, arrow-connected logic]
  "Was wirklich passiert:
  -> [mechanism 1]
  -> [mechanism 2]"

PROOF: [Personal experience OR client reference]
  "Ich sehe das jede Woche bei meinen Kundinnen."

SOLUTION: [Numbered steps OR exercise instructions]

CTA: [Keyword comment OR save/follow instruction]
  "Kommentiere [KEYWORD] und ich sende dir [offer]."

SIGNATURE (optional): "Deine Nicol 🤍"
```

Caption length: Min 500 chars. Standard 500-1000, educational 1000-2000.

## 12. Content-Type Modifiers

### Instagram Caption
- Full hook-body-CTA architecture
- Emoji as line-end accent, never mid-sentence
- Arrow (->) for cause-effect chains
- Keyword CTA at end
- Hashtags clustered at very end

### Reel Script (spoken)
- Even more fragmented sentences
- More colloquial: "Boah", "Mädel", "Bock", "mega"
- Direct camera address: "Ich zeig dir jetzt..."
- Ends with "Folge mir für mehr..." or "Probier's aus"
- Less scientific depth, more demonstration
- Intensifiers cluster: "ganz ganz wichtig", "mega wichtig", "sehr sehr sehr schön"
- Frequent "Bitte, bitte, bitte" for emphasis on critical points
- Self-referencing stats as authority: "Ich bin 53. 11% Körperfett. 22 Kilo Muskelmasse."
- Polarizing positions welcome: "Hör auf mit Cardio." is on-brand. Hedging is not.
- LIVE-session moments work as Reels: raw, emotional, unscripted segments where
  Nicol shares personal vulnerability (past trauma, body struggles, marriage) are
  among the strongest content because they feel authentic, not produced

### Email/Newsletter
- Warmer opening (less confrontational hook)
- "Hey {{firstname}}," standard, or personal name
- Longer explanatory passages acceptable
- "Deine Nicol 🤍" or "Alles Liebe / Deine Nicol 🤍" closing
- Links instead of keyword CTAs
- Slightly more polished sentence structure

**ON:** "Die meisten Frauen verschwenden ihre Zeit im Training. Nicht, weil sie faul sind. Nicht, weil sie zu wenig machen. Sondern weil sie das Falsche machen."
**OFF:** "Viele Frauen könnten ihre Trainingsergebnisse optimieren, indem sie ihre Übungsauswahl und Trainingsintensität überdenken und an ihre individuellen Bedürfnisse anpassen würden."

**ON:** "Dein Körper ist nicht kaputt. Er ist nur ehrlicher geworden."

**ON:** "Hör auf zu hungern. Fang an zu formen. ✨"

**ON:** "Körniger Frischkäse mit Zimt und Zucker. Ja, mit Zimt und Zucker. Das muss."
